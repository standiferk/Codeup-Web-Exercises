CIGARS-n-CRAFT BEER

These are a couple of my favorite things...especially together.

This page will be dedicated to pairing these two things for maximum enjoyment

CIGARS
I am well aware of the fact these are enjoyable on their own. Not trying to take anything away from cigars or craft beer; simply trying to find great pairings to really appreciate both together.




You are encouraged to:

1. Share you pairings. I am always looking for great pairings. It is understood most craft beers are local to a state if not a city. Please send info including where the cigs and beer can be found.

2. Try other's pairings. It is understood some travel may be involved, if you wish provide some contact info. If I'm coming to your area I'll buy the first beer.

3. There are other pairing sites out there but they are not interactive, hence they do not bring an under appreciated community together so please share as much info as possible using the "Share" and "Blog" options above.

CRAFT BEER
Craft beer...what can I say? If you're still drinking mass produced barley liquid best consumed at 28.8° you are missing out on an abundance of flavor.
